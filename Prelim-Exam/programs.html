Academic Programs

Our curriculum is designed in collaboration with industry leaders to ensure global competitiveness.

The BSCS program focuses on the concepts and theories of computing, algorithmic foundations, and implementation of software systems.

Specialization Tracks

  • Artificial Intelligence (AI)
  • Data Science & Analytics
  • Game Development

Career Outcomes

  • Software Engineer
  • Research Scientist
  • AI Developer

The BSIT program prepares students to design, implement, and manage IT infrastructure, including networks, web systems, and databases.

Specialization Tracks

  • Cybersecurity & Digital Forensics
  • Enterprise Web Engineering
  • Mobile Application Development

Career Outcomes

  • Network Administrator
  • Full-Stack Web Developer
  • IT Security Consultant

The BSIS program focuses on the design and implementation of solutions that integrate information technology with business processes.

Specialization Tracks

  • Enterprise Resource Planning
  • Business Process Outsourcing

Career Outcomes

  • Business Systems Analyst
  • Project Manager
  • Database Administrator

Industry Certifications

To boost employability, our students are prepared for global certifications including Cisco CCNA, CompTIA Security+, AWS Cloud Practitioner, and Oracle Java Certified Associate.
